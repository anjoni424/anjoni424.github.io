Tokenization

An LLM does not read a prompt like a human reading a sentence. It converts text into chunks called tokens, turns them into vectors, compares patterns, and predicts what token should come next.

Mini Objective

Keep the machine stable while still making progress. Too much noise raises confusion. Too little activity means no useful work gets done. Welcome to computing: organized panic in a nice shirt.
